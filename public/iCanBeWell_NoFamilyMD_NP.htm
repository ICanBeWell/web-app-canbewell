No Family Doctor or Nurse Practitioner

Here are some links to help you if:

  • You have a health problem now
  • You want to find a health care provider
If You Have a Health Problem

Dial 811 or go to the 811 website of your province, except in Manitoba go to Better Health.

When you go through 811, several provinces or territories can connect you with a nurse practitioner if you need a prescription or more advice.
